Headcount & Workforce Insights

This Excel dashboard summarizes HR metrics such as headcount, department distribution, salary trends, and hiring patterns to support workforce planning and reporting.

Key Features:

  • Headcount breakdown by department/team
  • Hiring trend analysis over time
  • Salary distribution and averages
  • Demographic splits (e.g., gender)
  • Interactive filtering using slicers
