Sales Performance Dashboard

Built an Excel dashboard to monitor performance across regions, products, and time. It helps stakeholders compare year-over-year results and identify top-performing segments at a glance.

Key Features:

  • Interactive PivotTables and PivotCharts for analysis
  • Slicers to filter by region, year, and product
  • Automated KPIs for quick reporting
  • Trend visuals highlighting growth patterns
